Network Working Group D. Walden Request for Comments: 473 BBN-NET NIC: 14811 28 February 1973 MIX and MIXAL? Is there an interpreter for Knuth's hypothetical computer, MIX, together with an assemble for the MIX assembly language, MIXAL, available on any ARPA Network Host? If not, will not some Network Host make them available (cheaply)?--it would be considerable service to all of us who are students of the "Art of Computer Programming."
