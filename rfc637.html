Network Working Group Alex A. McKenzie RFC # 637 BBN-NET NIC # 30519 April 23, 1974 CHANGE OF NETWORK ADDRESS FOR SU-DSL Effective immediately, the Host at Stanford Electronics Research Lab. (SU-DSL), a Very Distant Host connected to the SRI IMP, has its address changed from IMP 2, Host 2, to IMP 2 Host 3. Thus its "Network Address" is changed from 130 to 194.

Host 2 on IMP 2 will eventually be a PDP-11 operated by the SRI Artificial Intelligence Center.
