What is this?

RFCSpot is a website which hosts thousands of IETF Requests For Comments (RFCs) in an easy to understand two-column format. The left side of the page shows the content of the RFC and the right side shows related Stackoverflow questions.

See an example RFC or browse the full RFC list.

Who is behind this?

Hi, I'm Karim! I'm a backend engineer and have to spend a lot of time reading RFCs. Unfortunately, most of them are hard to understand because they look so dry. I decided to help my fellow engineers and write the RFC website I'd like to have. After a lot of on-and-off hacking, RFCSpot was born!

Special thanks:

In no specific order, thanks to the IETF, Stackoverflow and their contributors and Jeremy Ashkenas, who created the two column theme I'm using.

Comments, remarks?
